Usually no. The setup depends on your phone provider, but the goal is to fit around the current business number whenever possible.

Yes. The system can notify your team and hand off the lead once the customer is qualified or ready to book.

The writing can be natural and professional. The goal is not fake personality. The goal is speed, clarity, and lead capture.
